Saifee Events

Knowledge is prolific, when it brings people together.

Saifee Events brings more than 35 years of combined experience to be your agile Go-to partner for business trainings and conferences.

We will manage your on-going requirements of customised & certified training programs for your employees, and provide exclusive platforms to connect with clients & network with industry shapers.

What We Do

Conferences

We constantly invests in thorough research by connecting with global industry practitioners and thought leaders to curate innovative conferences that address business needs and challenges, explore and understand new trends and technologies to bring you the best practices. We provide an opportunity to establish business relations, enhance your brand awareness as well as to find international network for future collaborations. Our conferences programs and structure are designed keeping in mind our clients get maximum exposure to learning, networking and brand recognition.

Trainings

We choose internationally renowned technical experts and leading certified trainers that aids your career and personal development via industry recognised certifications and personal development programs. We also offer personalised in-house training courses that specifically address an organisation’s requirements by offering 100% customised program content and trainers to suit your objectives. Recognised and well planned training programs from the base for one’ career growth and knowledge development and here we take efforts to connect with both clients and trainers to understand the requirement for tomorrow and be prepared today.

Curated Events

Major corporations are in constant need to offer innovative platforms to communicate with their existing and potential clients. With our expertise and clientele, we specialise in bespoke seminars that are aimed just for you to connect with your target audience. We work with you to understand your event objective that helps us build the conference program and get the required delegates for your team of speakers at the event that is completely managed by us.
